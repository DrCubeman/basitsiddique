Hangman

General:

For this project, I had just finished a 4 Hour C++ Course, and wanted to build my first project.

I wanted it to be unique, although quite a bit of trouble developing an idea.

Although at Summer School, two people were playing a game of “Hangman” beside me.

And therefore the idea was born.

During Development:

Programming this game, had its challenges, the reslits from that and my limited C++ knowledge.

This ended up with crossroads away from the original Hangman’s gameplay.

Those being:

  • The maximum letters in a single word is 7
  • The entire word has to lowercase
  • A word if it contains a letter that appears more than once, this can only happen twice

    • Thanks to these limitations, that I set very late into development, I was able to make an easy system, that was a lot of comparisons, primarily, that basically, if the letter a user guessed

    equals a letter that is one of the inputted from earlier, then add a point or subtract a point if that’s not true.

    And I simply added a “and” feature that checked if a letter that is guessed, while equaling what the player guessed, also equals one of the other letters in the word, add two points.

    All-In-All:

    I learned a lot from this project.

    Sure, it took A LOT of time, and it’s very ugly to look at under the hood,

    But I’m still proud of the end project which looks way better.

    I was able to flily use the Pomodoro Technique, which allowed me to feel more relaxed while developing this project, knowing the time was something I didn’t need to replisively think about.

    In terms of code, I used things such as:

  • Primarily If statements, and else statements, alongside comparison operators
  • A lot of Booleans
  • Custom functions
  • Arrays

    • And Basics such as strings, and ints.

    I’m happy I was able to start and stay with a project, no matter how tired I colid sometimes be.

    And I plan in the future to remake this project, hopeflily with more knowledge of C++, and a cleaner engine under the hood.
